## Description

Tall, dark-haired human woman. Striking and sensual, with a hint of danger that is not a hint at all. A flowing cloak and a [force pike](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Force_pike/Legends) are her constants. She greets enemies with a chilling smile.

## Background

Not much is known about her origins. She has teamed with [Kast](jodo-kast.md) for the thrill — the money is secondary, though she won't turn it down. The hunt is everything. She loves personal combat and takes extraordinary risks, but not stupid ones — her one fear is dying senselessly.
