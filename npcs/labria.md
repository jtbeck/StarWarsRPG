## Description

Tall humanoid with a diabolical face — razor-sharp teeth, two large horns jutting from his bald head. Slurred speech, stumbling gait. Looks like a villain. Acts like a town drunk. Is, in fact, both.

## Background

A knowledge broker who has made a living in [Mos Eisley](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Mos_Eisley/Legends)'s shadows for years — knowing things, connecting people, staying just useful enough to stay alive. He decided to work for [Jodo Kast](jodo-kast.md) for the money. He wants to live to a ripe old age and decided this was the way to do it.

He was the messenger who lured the PCs into the Cantina Ambush in Episode 4, pretending to be passing along Old Arno's message.

## Personality

Despite his frightening appearance, genuinely a coward and a drunk. He is useful, not dangerous. Will fold immediately under real pressure. Working for Kast because he was scared not to.
