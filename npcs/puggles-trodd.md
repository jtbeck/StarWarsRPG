## Description

Short, furry, rodent-like alien. Sharp teeth, long tail, pointed ears. Sounds perpetually aggrieved. He loves watching things explode and hates being anywhere near the explosion himself.

## Background

Joined [Kast](jodo-kast.md) and [Zardra](zardra.md) for the money. He's afraid of both of them, but working for the Empire keeps a person eating. He would rather throw a grenade from a rooftop than be anywhere near actual fighting.

## Equipment

Hold-out blaster (3D+1), knife, 4 grenades (5D), comlink, 5 blocks detonite (1D each), various fuses.

## Personality

Unpleasant, brooding, pessimistic. He complains constantly. He loves explosions — loves causing them, hates being near them. Will duck out of any direct confrontation if he can.
