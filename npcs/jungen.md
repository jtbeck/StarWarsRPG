## Description

Huge, hulking, reptilian male with a bony-ridged head. Deep, rumbling voice. Quiet and deliberate. Prefers hand-to-hand combat to blasters. His armor makes him slower but much harder to hurt.

## Background

A young enlistee aboard Raster's flagship. In his short time under Raster's command, he grew to respect the old commander deeply. When it looked like the Emperor would have Raster killed, Jungen pledged his protection. That pledge has not faded in all the years since. He has no qualms about stealing from the corrupt, and always goes out of his way to help those in need.
