## Description

Young and ambitious. Wears the battered, battle-scarred armor of a [Mandalorian](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Mandalorian_armor/Legends) warrior — patterned deliberately after [Boba Fett](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Boba_Fett/Legends). Cold eyes. Economical movements. He took this job to make a name for himself. Every prey he takes is a step toward the reputation he craves.

## Background

Hired by Captain Parlan of the *Relentless* to find and capture Graff Raster alive. He assembled a team — [Zardra](zardra.md) and [Puggles Trodd](puggles-trodd.md) — and seeded [Mos Eisley](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Mos_Eisley/Legends) with agents and bounty hunters to flush out any leads. He has been methodically eliminating anyone who might know Raster's location, then moving on before the Rebels can catch up.

Of all his weapons, he enjoys his poison darts most. Each is coated with a different compound — some paralyze, some kill.

## Personality

Cold. Calculating. Danger is not something he feels — it's something he creates in others. He is not reckless. He will not appear until the odds favor him.

> *"Watching prey squirm gives me a feeling of absolute power."*
