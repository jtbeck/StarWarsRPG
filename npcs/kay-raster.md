## Description

Elegant, composed. Her touch is visible throughout [Tusken Fort](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Tusken_Fort) — the indoor garden, the furnishings, the care put into making a fortress feel like a home. She stands by her husband's side when the PCs arrive.

## Background

Married Graff Raster during his years in hiding on [Tatooine](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Tatooine/Legends). She knows exactly who he is and what he was — and chose this life with him anyway. She is not a fighter, but she is not fragile.
