## Description

Battered chrome Droid, tall and thin. Covered in built-in and externally-mounted weapons. Pretends to be an enforcement Droid going about routine business. True nature: bring death to all living things.

## Background

Assigned long ago to find Graff Raster. When ordered to accept a memory wipe afterward, IG-72 refused and went rogue. It has been operating independently since — taking contracts, conserving energy, waiting for a mission it could commit to fully. When the Empire posted the bounty on Raster, the Droid's old objective reactivated. Dead bounties are easier to collect than live ones.
