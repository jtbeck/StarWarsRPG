## Description

Looks much like the street preacher from [Mos Eisley](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Mos_Eisley/Legends) — cleaner, better dressed, wearing the calm authority of someone who leads by genuine example rather than performance. He listens fully before he speaks.

## The Dim-U

The [Dim-U](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Dim-U) are a religious order devoted to the mystery of the [Bantha](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Bantha/Legends). They believe the sacred animal has been placed throughout the galaxy as a welcoming presence — a living sign that any world can be a home. They preach against killing Banthas and maintain communal relationships with the great beasts. They respect [Tusken Raiders](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Tusken_Raider/Legends) as the chosen people of the Bantha on [Tatooine](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Tatooine/Legends).

## Role in Episode 5

Receives the party at the Oasis with genuine hospitality. Doesn't know where Graff Raster is hidden. Sent bounty hunters on their way earlier — *anger is not the Bantha's way.* After [Zardra's](zardra.md) attack, he thanks the PCs and offers food, water, and two medpacs. Extends an open invitation to return.
