## Description

Grizzled, sun-baked, craggy-faced from constant exposure to [Tatooine](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Tatooine/Legends)'s suns. Long bristly beard. He surveys everything calmly and speaks in a slow drawl. Carries himself like a man who has survived the desert for decades because he respected it.

## Background

One of the last of [Tatooine](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Tatooine/Legends)'s original colonists — passengers on the ship whose wreckage still sits at the center of [Mos Eisley](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Mos_Eisley/Legends). He has worked the desert his whole life as a scout, guide, and inspector. His oldest friends — Heff and Slag Flats — have now been murdered. He won't rest until their killer is found.

He doesn't fully trust the party yet. But he trusts them more than the bounty hunters, and he knows the Wastes better than anyone alive.
