**STAR WARS**

**Episode V**

**THE JAILBREAK**

The galaxy bleeds under the iron grip of the GALACTIC EMPIRE. On the desert world of TATOOINE, a desperate search is underway. Rebel agents have traveled far to find GRAFF RASTER — a fallen Jedi and the last hope of the Alliance — before Imperial hunters close the trap.

The night of their arrival, violence erupted in a CANTINA ambush. Bounty hunters lay dead or fled. The lethal JODO KAST vanished into the shadows. And the desert scout OLD ARNO stepped from the darkness, offering to lead the party into the wastes at dawn.

But the night's violence has claimed an unexpected casualty. HENRY — the adolescent Wookiee ward of the engineer GUNE — flew into a berserker rage and ran howling into the streets of MOS EISLEY. The militia found him standing over the wreckage.

Now Henry sits in an Eisley jail cell. The crew of the CENTENNIAL DUCK has until dawn to get him out.
