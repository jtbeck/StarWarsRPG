## The Jail

A converted stone storage vault attached to the **Regional Government Office** (Location #11 on the Mos Eisley map). Front office and a rear cell block — three cells cut from the same rock as the city.

**Staff on duty:**
- 2 militiamen in the front office at all times
- 1 [stormtrooper](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Stormtrooper/Legends) posted outside the front door
- **[Prefect Orun Depp](../npcs/prefect-depp.md)** — sole keyholder, lives two streets east, not present overnight

**The Cells:**

| Cell | Occupant | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **[Henry](../pcs/henry.md)** | Working the bars for hours |
| 2 | **[Gamorrean PC](../pcs/gamorrean.md)** | Thrown in to fight Henry. Didn't. |
| 3 | **Baz** | Weathered smuggler. Knows the layout. Wants out. |

**What actually happened in Cell 1/2:** The [Gamorrean](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Gamorrean/Legends) took one look at the terrified young [Wookiee](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Wookiee/Legends) and sat down. You don't fight something that's already beaten. By arrival, both have identified the weakest bar and been working it together. Almost loose.

## The Approaches

### Option A — The Bribe
[Prefect Depp](../npcs/prefect-depp.md) talks tough but folds under pressure.

- Finding Depp: Easy *streetwise* or ask any local
- Bribe Depp: Opposed *bargain* — **500 credits** or invoke [Jabba](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Jabba_Desilijic_Tiure/Legends)'s name convincingly
- Bribe militia: 100 credits each — they'll look away but can't release without Depp's keys
- **Best angle:** Frame it as Jabba's business. [Akkik](../npcs/akkik.md) and [Gorrt](../npcs/gorrt.md) work for Jabba. The militia knows this.

### Option B — The Distraction
Draw the guards out, slip someone to the cell block.

- Engineer a brawl nearby: Easy *con* or *streetwise*
- The stormtrooper won't leave his post but will radio in — **6-round clock** starts
- Cell lock: **Moderate *dexterity*** (improvised tools) or **Moderate *Technical*** (proper tools)
- **Gune** is the ideal candidate — best tech skills and the only one Henry will stay quiet for

### Option C — The Inside Man
One PC surrenders deliberately.

- Bora mouthing off to the stormtrooper works cleanly
- Placed near Henry's cell. Good character moment — take the time.
- Outside team runs distraction/bribe while inside team coordinates

### Option D — Direct Force
Not recommended.

- 2 militia + 1 stormtrooper inside; stormtrooper signal brings 2 more in 4 rounds, then 2 more every 2 rounds up to 10 (Martial Law rules, module p.4)
- Prefect Depp becomes a persistent problem for the rest of the adventure
