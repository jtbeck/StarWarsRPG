## Encounter 1 — Here There Be Dragons

**Location:** Open desert flat, mid-morning.

A sail barge stranded and under attack from three [Krayt Dragons](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Krayt_dragon/Legends). Six bumbling amateur bounty hunters aboard — not Kast's crew.

> *As you come over a large sand dune you see a flat plain that stretches to the horizon. There, lying wounded just above the burning sand, is a large sail barge. Overloaded with bumbling, amateur bounty hunters, the craft appears to be ripe pickings for the creatures surrounding it. The sail barge is under attack by three huge, terrifying monsters! "Them's Krayt Dragons," explains Arno, "some of the most fearsome creatures to walk the Tatooine sands."*

**Choice:** Help or drive past. Helping earns Arno's approval; ungrateful hunters try to steal the speeder if rescued.

## Encounter 2 — Sedi Fisk's Desert Manor

**Location:** Rocky canyon settlement. Mid-day.

> *The canyon is quiet, still. Nothing moves but the hot, midday breeze. The manor lies in ruins, as does every other dwelling in the vicinity. Whatever happened here occurred long ago.*

**Search:** Easy *Perception/search* — ruins at least a year old. Fresh animal droppings.

> *You realize things are moving in the darkened corners. A shadow disconnects from a crumbling wall and dashes in your direction.*

Five [Womp Rats](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Womp_rat/Legends) attack.

## Encounter 3 — The Oasis

**Location:** [Dim-U](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Dim-U) [Bantha](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Bantha/Legends) priest community. First Twilight, Day 3.

A small desert settlement — humble dwellings, moisture farms, free-roaming [Banthas](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Bantha/Legends). The [Dim-U](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Dim-U) respect [Sand People](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Tusken_Raider/Legends) as the chosen of the Bantha and maintain a careful peace.

> *It is after dark when the Rebels arrive. A priest greets them and directs them to the main hall. Along the way children play atop huge [Banthas](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Bantha/Legends), farmers finish work, community members go about their evening.*

**[High Priest Dryon](../npcs/dryon.md)** receives them. He does not know where Graff Raster is, but [Tusken Fort](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Tusken_Fort) is the last name on Arno's list.

> *"Oasis doesn't ask for names or explanations. Like the humble [Bantha](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Bantha/Legends), we simply greet newcomers and offer what aid we can."*

### The Night Attack

> *Your peaceful evening ends abruptly as a window shatters and a smoke grenade crashes against the far wall. Dark smoke fills the hall. As citizens flee into the night, blaster fire cuts them down.*
>
> *"You will tell us what [Jodo Kast](../npcs/jodo-kast.md) wants to know, Dryon — or we will raze Oasis so that not even a speck of sand remains!"*

[Zardra](../npcs/zardra.md) leads five seasoned hunters. She doesn't know the PCs are here. Once she realizes she's facing real opposition she switches to *"win or die"* mode. Hunters fight to the death.

**After:** Dryon offers food, water, and two medpacs. *"May you always travel in the shadow of the [Bantha](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Bantha/Legends)."*
