## Campaign Modifications

> These override the published module everywhere.

- **[Adar Tallon](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Adar_Tallon) = Graff Raster** throughout. He is a **fallen [Jedi](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Jedi/Legends)**, not merely a military commander.
- **Captain Bora and Indie are siblings.** Graff Raster is their estranged uncle. They do not yet know he is the man they are looking for. This reveal is reserved for their meeting at Tusken Fort.
- **Henry** (adolescent [Wookiee](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Wookiee/Legends)) is a new party NPC, Gune's ward.
- **New [Gamorrean](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Gamorrean/Legends) PC** — former [Jabba](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Jabba_Desilijic_Tiure/Legends) guard, introduced via the Jailbreak episode.
- Episode 4 ended with Henry's Wookiee rage during the Cantina Ambush, leading to his arrest.

# THE JAILBREAK
### New Episode — Night of Day 2

---

## Previously On...

The Cantina Ambush is over. Bounty hunters are dead or fled. [Jodo Kast](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Jodo_Kast) fired a poison dart from the shadows and vanished. Old Arno stepped out of the darkness — the last surviving oldster, the desert scout who knew Graff Raster — and offered to lead the party into the [Tatooine](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Tatooine/Legends) Wastes at Second Dawn.

But during the fight, Henry went into a Wookiee rage. He ran into the street swinging. The militia arrived to find an adolescent [Wookiee](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Wookiee/Legends) and a scene of carnage. They took him in.

**Henry is in the [Mos Eisley](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Mos_Eisley/Legends) jail. The party needs him out before dawn.**

---

## The Jail

A converted stone storage vault attached to the **Regional Government Office** (Location #11, Mos Eisley map). Two rooms: a front office and a rear cell block with three cells cut from the same rock as the city.

**Staff:**
- 2 militiamen in the front office at all times
- 1 Imperial [stormtrooper](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Stormtrooper/Legends) posted outside the front door
- **Prefect Orun Depp** — sole keyholder. Not present overnight. Lives two streets east.

**Cell Block:**
- **Cell 1:** Henry ([Wookiee](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Wookiee/Legends)). Has been quietly working the bars for hours.
- **Cell 2:** The new [Gamorrean](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Gamorrean/Legends) PC — arrested in the general chaos after the cantina fight. The militia threw him in with Henry hoping they'd fight to the death and solve both problems at once.
- **Cell 3:** Baz — a weathered human smuggler. Wrong place, wrong time. Knows the jail layout and guard rotation. Will share everything for a ride out of [Mos Eisley](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Mos_Eisley/Legends).

**What actually happened in Cell 1/2:** The [Gamorrean](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Gamorrean/Legends) took one look at the terrified young [Wookiee](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Wookiee/Legends) and sat down. You don't fight something that's already beaten — that's not honor. By the time the PCs arrive, the two have identified the weakest bar in the cell and have been working it together. Almost loose. They can't quite finish it.

---

## The Approaches

### Option A: The Bribe
**The Prefect** is a coward who "talks tough but can be easily bullied or bribed."

- Finding Depp's home: Easy *streetwise* roll, or ask any local
- Bribing Depp directly: Opposed *bargain* roll. Release fee: **500 credits**, or a convincing argument. [Jabba](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Jabba_Desilijic_Tiure/Legends)'s name is a strong argument — *"This Wookiee is Jabba's property and you're causing an incident."*
- Bribing the militia: 100 credits each. They'll look the other way but cannot release prisoners without Depp's keys.
- **Best angle:** The [Gamorrean](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Gamorrean/Legends)'s connection to [Jabba](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Jabba_Desilijic_Tiure/Legends). [Akkik](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Akkik) and [Gorrt](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Gorrt) work for Jabba. The militia knows this. Framing the Gamorrean's release as "Jabba's business" makes them very eager to cooperate.

### Option B: The Distraction
Create chaos elsewhere. Draw the guards out.

- [Mos Eisley](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Mos_Eisley/Legends) still has a few jumpy bounty hunters in it. One shove in the right cantina and the street erupts. **Easy *con* or *streetwise*** roll to engineer a believable incident.
- The [stormtrooper](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Stormtrooper/Legends) won't abandon his post for a brawl — but he will call it in, starting a **6-round clock** before more troopers arrive.
- With the militia gone, one PC slips to the cell block window. The cell lock is old hardware: **Moderate *dexterity*** roll with improvised tools, or **Moderate *Technical*** with proper tools, to pop it.
- **Gune's moment:** Gune is by far the best candidate for the lock job. Also the only one Henry will stay quiet for.

### Option C: The Inside Man
One PC gets arrested on purpose.

- Captain Bora mouthing off to the [stormtrooper](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Stormtrooper/Legends) would do it efficiently.
- Inside, they're placed in Cell 3 with Baz (or near Henry's cell if the GM wants drama).
- **Character beat:** Bora alone with a scared [Wookiee](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Wookiee/Legends) kid, knowing somewhere in this desert is the uncle he never knew. Good moment. Take the time.
- Outside team works the distraction/bribe while Bora coordinates from inside.

### Option D: Direct Force
Not recommended. But players will be players.

- 2 militiamen, 1 [stormtrooper](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Stormtrooper/Legends), stone walls.
- If the stormtrooper signals: **4 rounds** until 2 more troopers arrive, then **2 more every 2 rounds** up to 10 total (Martial Law, per module p.4).
- Prefect Depp will be a persistent problem for the rest of the adventure.

## Encounter 1 — Here There Be Dragons

**Location:** Open desert flat, mid-morning.

A large sail barge is stranded and under attack by three [Krayt Dragons](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Krayt_dragon/Legends). Six bumbling amateur bounty hunters are aboard — rank amateurs who answered the Imperial bounty, not Kast's crew.

> *As you come over a large sand dune you see a flat plain that stretches to the horizon. There, lying wounded just above the burning sand, is a large sail barge. Overloaded with bumbling, amateur bounty hunters, the craft appears to be ripe pickings for the creatures surrounding it. The sail barge is under attack by three huge, terrifying monsters! "Them's Krayt Dragons," explains Arno, "some of the most fearsome creatures to walk the Tatooine sands."*

**Choice:** Help or drive past.
- If they help: the grateful hunters try to commandeer the PCs' speeder afterward. Easy to ditch.
- If they ignore: describe the barge going down behind them on the horizon.

## Encounter 2 — Sedi Fisk's Desert Manor

**Location:** Rocky canyon settlement. Mid-day.

> *The canyon is quiet, still. Nothing moves but the hot, midday breeze, and it blows unobstructed through and around the shattered buildings. The manor lies in ruins, as does every other dwelling in the vicinity. Whatever happened here occurred long ago. It appears Sedi Fisk no longer lives in this tiny community.*

**Search:** Easy *Perception* or *search* — ruins at least a year old. Some fresh animal droppings.

> *The broken remains lean toward you as you climb over rubble and under hanging wreckage. After a time the twin suns dip lower and shadows grow longer. You realize things are moving in the darkened corners, just out of sight. A shadow disconnects from a crumbling wall and dashes in your direction.*

Five [Womp Rats](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Womp_rat/Legends) attack.

## Encounter 3 — The Oasis

**Location:** [Dim-U](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Dim-U) [Bantha](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Bantha/Legends) priest community. First Twilight, Day 3.

Arno leads them here for shelter and information. A small, humble settlement — moisture farms, stone dwellings. [Banthas](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Bantha/Legends) roam freely. Friendly, generous people who respect [Tusken Raiders](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Tusken_Raider/Legends) as the chosen of the Bantha.

> *It is after dark when the Rebels arrive. A priest greets them and directs them to the main hall where they can find food and shelter. Along the way children play atop huge [Banthas](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Bantha/Legends), farmers finish their work, and community members go about various activities.*

**High Priest Dryon** receives them. He sent bounty hunters on their way earlier — anger is not the Bantha's way. He genuinely does not know where Graff Raster is hiding, but [Tusken Fort](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Tusken_Fort) is the last stop.

> *"As I told the others earlier, Oasis doesn't ask for names or explanations. Like the humble [Bantha](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Bantha/Legends), we simply greet newcomers and offer what aid we can to make life easier upon this suns-scorched ball."*

### The Night Attack

> *Your peaceful evening ends abruptly as a window shatters and a smoke grenade crashes against the far wall. Dark, noxious smoke quickly fills the hall, and people begin to rush from the building. As they get out into the night, blaster fire smashes through the darkness and citizens of Oasis fall. Then a voice calls out: "You will tell us what [Jodo Kast](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Jodo_Kast) wants to know, Dryon, or we will raze Oasis so that not even a speck of sand remains!"*

**[Zardra](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Zardra)** leads five seasoned hunters. She doesn't know the PCs are here. Once she realizes she's facing real fighters she switches to *"glorious night to win or die"* mode. Hunters fight to the death.

**After:** Dryon offers food, water, two medpacs. Open invitation to return. *"May you always travel in the shadow of the [Bantha](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Bantha/Legends)."*
