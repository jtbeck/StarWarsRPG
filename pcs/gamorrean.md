## How He Got Here

A [Gamorrean](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Gamorrean/Legends) guard on [Jabba's](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Jabba_Desilijic_Tiure/Legends) payroll. During the chaos of the Cantina Ambush, he waded into the fight — because there was a fight and he was in it. In the process, he put [Gorrt](../npcs/gorrt.md) through a table. [Akkik](../npcs/akkik.md) reported him to Jabba immediately, framing it as going rogue.

Jabba fired him via comlink — in front of the militia who were arriving on scene. No employer protection. No credentials. Arrested in the general chaos.

## The Cell

The militia threw him in with [Henry](henry.md) hoping they'd fight to the death. He took one look at a terrified adolescent Wookiee pressed into a corner and sat down. You don't finish something that's already over — that's not honor. By the time the PCs arrive, both have been quietly working the cell bars together for hours.
