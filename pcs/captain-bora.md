## Description

Dark hair, beard. Wears a brown leather jacket. Experienced, roguish — the look of someone who has made a living in the spaces between Imperial law and actual crime, and found it comfortable there.

## Character Notes

Bora is the one the party looks to when things go sideways. He's good under pressure and better at talking his way into and out of things than he usually admits.

*The uncle reveal is reserved for the face-to-face at [Tusken Fort](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Tusken_Fort). How Bora processes that moment — and what it means for his commitment to the mission — is one of the emotional centers of the adventure.*
