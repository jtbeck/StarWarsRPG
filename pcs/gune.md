## Description

Small, furry, large-pointed ears, goggles, brown robes, utility equipment everywhere. Cheerful expression that borders on mischievous. Looks harmless. Is not useless.

## Relationships

| | |
|---|---|
| **[Henry](henry.md)** | Gune rescued Henry from slavery. Henry follows Gune completely. In the jail, Henry will only stay calm when he hears Gune's voice. |
| **[Captain Bora](captain-bora.md)** | Crewmate. Bora trusts Gune's technical instincts without question. |
| **[Indie](indie.md)** | The ship is Indie's to fly and Gune's to keep alive. They have an understanding. |

## Character Notes

Gune's relationship with [Henry](henry.md) is central to the Jailbreak episode. He's the only one who can calm Henry mid-rage. The lock-picking approach in the jailbreak is built around his skills.
