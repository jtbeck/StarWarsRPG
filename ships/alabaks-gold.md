## Overview

The party's ship. A converted [Mon Calamari](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Mon_Calamari/Legends) freighter, slightly smaller than the *Millennium Falcon*. Currently docked at [Docking Bay 94](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Docking_Bay_94/Legends) in [Mos Eisley](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Mos_Eisley/Legends).
