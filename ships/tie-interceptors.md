## Overview

Three [TIE/IN Interceptors](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/TIE/IN_interceptor/Legends) scramble to intercept the party when they break atmosphere in Episode 8. Their goal is not to destroy — it's to *lightly damage* the ships and prevent escape to hyperspace while the *Relentless* moves into tractor beam range.

## Tactics

The TIEs use speed, combined fire, and evasion. They attempt to lightly damage the Rebels' ships to slow them — not destroy them. Parlan wants Raster taken alive.

**Escape condition:** If the Rebel ships increase range to greater than long range, or survive 10 rounds of combat, they move out of [Tatooine](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Tatooine/Legends)'s gravity well and can jump to hyperspace.

**Failure condition:** If *Alabak's Gold* is destroyed or rolls a 1 or 3 on the "Starship System Damage Table," the *Relentless* arrives and captures the ships with tractor beams.

## The *Relentless*

The [Star Destroyer](https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Imperial_I-class_Star_Destroyer/Legends) drops out of hyperspace just as the Rebels are trying to run. Parlan has a hologram conversation with Darth Vader before arrival — Vader reminds him not to fail the Emperor. Play this cinematic cut before the space battle begins.
